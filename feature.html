Graphic design focuses on communicating a message visually. The message may be to provide information,
invoke thought, sell a product or even stimulate meaningful conversation. Graphic design is all around you. Itâ€™s about

The popularization of social media and the advancements in technology have sparked curiosity about graphic
design. The ability to easily share information is leading to an increasing number of artists looking to share their work
and engage with new audiences. Technology provides opportunities for the most novice of art lovers to share their
artwork.
Graphic design is the use of color, shape, layout, typography, and other elements to create and promote a
message. At its best, it combines scientific communication principles with aesthetics to resonate with its audience and
provoke action and/or emotion. It is the art of VISUAL COMMUNICATION through the use of text, images, symbols and artwork.
